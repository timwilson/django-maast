Welcome to the MAA Score Tabulator (M.A.A.S.T.) project

This website has every score from every archery tournament run by the Minnesota Archers Alliance going back more than 20 years. At last count that’s over 11,000 scores at 127 tournaments resulting in almost 900 individual state records.

The information on the site is extensively linked. Once you’re viewing a table of results, try clicking on some data and seeing where it takes you. Use the search at the top of the page to find individual archers’ scores and any state records or podium finishes they’ve earned.
