Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Minnesota Archers Alliance state record database website. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of information for users visiting our website.

2. Information Collection

Our website does not track or collect any personal information from our users. However, we reserve the right to use technology to monitor website traffic and general usage data. The purpose of this data collection is solely to analyze which features of our site are most popular and to aid in the development of new features that may benefit our visitors.

3. Use of Collected Information

The aggregated data collected is used for internal purposes only, such as understanding how our visitors use the website and which sections are most frequented. This helps us in enhancing the user experience and in planning future improvements.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any data collected from our website with third parties. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no private data about individuals to disclose.

5. Data Security

As our website does not store any private data about individuals, the risk of data breach is minimal. However, we are committed to ensuring the security of our website and its users.

6. User Rights and Contact Information

For any corrections to the data presented on our website, users can email us at president@themnaa.org. If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy while using our website, please contact us at privacy@themnaa.org.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website does not create any cookies or use tracking technologies, other than the minimal data collection technologies mentioned above for tracking site usage.

8. International Users

The MAA State Record Database is designed for users within the United States and does not serve users from outside the U.S.

9. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
